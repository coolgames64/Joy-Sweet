糖水 · sweet soup

A bowl to soften the day

“解忧食糖” means a little sweetness to ease your worries — and that’s really the whole idea. In Cantonese kitchens, 糖水 are gentle, slow-simmered sweet soups you turn to for comfort: warming when it’s cold, cooling when it’s warm, and always a little nourishing.

We make ours the unhurried way — tofu pudding set fresh, taro balls rolled by hand, red beans cooked down slow — then add a few ideas of our own, from mango snow mountains to coconut served in the shell. Find us across from Multea Choice on Faulkner Street, or let us bring a bowl to your door.
