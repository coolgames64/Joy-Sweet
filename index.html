解忧 · to ease your worries

A little sweetness, made by hand

“解忧食糖” means sweets to ease your worries. In Cantonese kitchens, 糖水 are gentle, slow-simmered sweet soups you turn to for comfort — warming when it’s cold, cooling when it’s warm. We make ours fresh each day, then add a few ideas of our own.
